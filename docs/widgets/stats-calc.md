## Explanation of Controls

To divide x by y, for instance, enter _y_, click _/_, enter _x_, then click _=_.

A few of the button labels might be unfamiliar.

- _nCk_ is the number of combinations of n things taken k at a time. To compute it, enter n, click _nCk_, enter k, then click _=_.
- _nPk_ is the number of permutations of k of n things.
- _U[0,1]_ returns a pseudorandom number between zero and one.
